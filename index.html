OUR VISION

PROPAGANDA #084

OFFICIAL ELECTORAL POSTER · VOTE FOR COMPLETE NONSENSE

1. WHY THE SWARM EXISTS

The system runs on the sweat of sleep-deprived individuals chasing corporate ladder milestones. We, the Cockroach Janta Party, propose an alternative pathway: surviving by hiding in the cracks, drinking tea, and waiting for the capitalist structures to inevitably crash due to their own meetings.

2. ANTI-CORRUPTION (BY INACTION)

How can we take bribes if we are too lazy to open the envelope? By refusing to do any productive work, we automatically eliminate 100% of corruption risks. If there's no project to deliver, there's no money to steal. It's clean, efficient, and requires zero effort.

3. YOUTH FRUSTRATION & MEMES

Our political strategy is built around visual memes, viral reels, and comment-section warfare. The battlefield is no longer the streets; it is the replies under corporate announcements. Our weapon of choice is sarcasm, and our shield is chronic internet availability.

01

Universal Basic Wi-Fi

Speed is a Human Right

A mandatory, constitutionally guaranteed 100 Mbps Wi-Fi connection for every citizen, with no data caps and zero blockages on social media, streaming sites, or doomscrolling platforms.

"How can we discuss nation-building when the video is buffering at 480p? A slow internet is the ultimate form of state-sponsored oppression."
NON-NEGOTIABLE
02

Mandatory Nap Hours

Snooze to Progress

All public and private organizations must observe a nation-wide sleep window from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Sending Slack notifications, emails, or calls during this time will be treated as a criminal offense.

"Scientific studies conducted by our sleeping committee prove that 99% of bad political decisions are made when people are awake. Let's sleep for the nation."
CRITICAL SLEEP
03

Professional Ranting Stipend

Pay for the Discontent

Every registered citizen spending over 6 hours daily ranting about system errors, price hikes, or societal norms on Twitter/X, Reddit, or tea stalls shall receive a monthly Ranting Stipend.

"Constructive complaining is high-value emotional labor. If the government won't fix the issues, the least they can do is subsidize our internet rants."
FUNDED BY LAZINESS
04

Subsidized Chai & Maggi

Fueling the Swarm

Setting a permanent price cap of ₹5 for a hot cup of ginger-cardamom Chai and ₹10 for a standard plate of double-masala instant noodles at all locations.

"Chai and Maggi are the twin pillars of youth survival. If food is a right, then street-side gourmet instant meals are a fundamental necessity."
SWARM FUEL
05

Ban 'Any Update?' Emails

Let It Be

Ban managers, clients, or team leads from sending follow-up messages containing phrases like 'Any update?', 'Just checking in', or 'Status?'. Work will be delivered when it is done, or never.

"Pressure creates diamonds, but we are cockroaches, not minerals. Micromanagement will be declared a human rights violation."
ABOLISHED FOREVER
